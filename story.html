Chapter 01

When I first saw you

Jab maine tumhe pehli baar dekha, main SPR tha — ProC DNA interview ka time tha, aur tumhari phati hui thi. Sach bolun? Pehli baar shart haar ke mera faida hua tha… because that moment gave me you. Tab se tum meri favorite distraction ho.

Chapter 02

Our first walk together

Uske baad main tumse baat karne ka bahana dhoondhne laga. Flirt karne ki koshish toh karta tha, par dhang se ho hi nahi paata tha — dar lagta tha ki kahin tum mujhe chepu na samajh lo. Aur jab tumne pehli baar walk ke liye invite kiya na, main itna khush tha… aur utna hi dara hua bhi, ki tum judge toh nahi karogi. Kya bolun, kya karun — pura system hang. Main legit do baar nahaya tha us din… phir bhi kapde fuddu hi pehen ke gaya. But somehow, you still made that day feel perfect.

Chapter 03

That month you were away

Phir walk ke baad jo tumne mujhe ignore kiya, bhai… that was the scariest phase. Walk ke kuch din baad hi tum ghar chali gayi, aur usse pehle mujhse na milne ke bahane banne lage. Mujhe toh laga tha tum mere hi wajah se ghar gayi ho. The month you tried to ignore me — and I pretended it didn’t affect me. (It did. A lot.) Lucky for me, main is type ki cheezon mein thoda dumb hoon… isliye baat karta raha. Aur at last, pat gayi meri favorite ladki.

Chapter 04

Our first kiss

Phir woh din aaya jab tum ghar se wapas aayi — 25th October. Bhaiiiii, sabse crazy day. Ussi din humari relationship start hui, aur woh moment main kabhi nahi bhool sakta. Our first kiss… tum jo halka sa kaap rahi thi, that look, that pause — sab kuch still replay hota hai dimaag mein. Uske baad tum thodi confuse hui thi, but main full sure tha: I have my Janeman now. And suddenly… there was no confusion anymore. Just us. Aur sach bolun, us din ke baad duniya thodi kam boring aur tum thodi zyada meri lagne lagi.

Chapter 05

Our Gamebox dates

And Gamebox date? One of the best moments, hands down. Tumhare saath pool khelna, Mortal Kombat mein ladna, aur jab main haarta tha toh tumhara victory dance — that was the bestttt. Not fancy. Not planned. Just laughter, chaos, thoda sa drama, and you — which made it perfect.
